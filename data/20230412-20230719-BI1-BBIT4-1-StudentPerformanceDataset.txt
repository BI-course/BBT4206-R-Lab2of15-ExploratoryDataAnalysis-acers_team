The "BI 1 Student Performance Dataset" was collected between August and September 2023.
It provides 101 observations and 100 variables of data about the Bachelor of Business
Information Technology (BBIT) students at Strathmore University, Kenya.
The data concerns the students' performance during the 
"BBT4106: Business Intelligence I" course offered between 12th April and 19th July 2023.

Contact person: Allan Omondi
Email: aomondi_at_strathmore_dot_edu

Below is metadata of some of the variables in the dataset:

class_group: The class is divided into 3 groups and the student is either in group A, B, or C.
gender: 1 = Male, 0 = Female
YOB: Year of Birth
regret_choosing_bi: B.1. Do you regret choosing the Business Intelligence option instead of the computer networking option? - Yes = 1, No = 0
drop_bi_now: B.2. If given a chance now, would you drop the Business Intelligence option for the computer networking option? - Yes = 1, No = 0
motivator: B.3. Which of the following motivates you? - The desire to succeed = 1, The fear of failure = 0

Rating scale: 1 = Strongly disagree, 5 = Strongly agree
read_content_before_lecture: C.1. I always read the content prior to a lecture
anticipate_test_questions: C.2. I anticipate test questions and their answers as I read.
answer_rhetorical_questions: C.3. I answer rhetorical questions in my mind during lectures to test my retention of the reading.
find_terms_I_do_not_know: C.4. I find terms I do not know, and I re-learn those terms.
copy_new_terms_in_reading_notebook: C.5. I copy new terms in a reading notebook and make sure I understand them.
take_quizzes_and_use_results: C.6 I take quizzes and use the results to understand what I do not know.
reorganise_course_outline: C.7. I reorganise the course outline into a study guide.
write_down_important_points: C.8. I write down points that are important and test my understanding of the points periodically.
space_out_revision: C.9. I space out my revision over the duration of the semester.
studying_in_study_group: C.10. I do most of my studying in a study group.
schedule_appointments: C.11. I schedule appointments during the lecturer's office hours to ask questions that I have about the course.
goal_oriented: C.12. Do you have written down or typed (not the same as mental) SMART goals that you review at least every week?" - Yes = 1, No = 0

C.13. Rate the following study techniques/methods according to how frequently you apply it.
Rating scale: 1 = Never, 2 = Rarely, 3 = Frequently, 4 = Always

interleaving: C.13.c. [Interleaving: https://youtu.be/k5A26Sc63F0]
categorizing: C.13.d. [Categorizing: https://youtu.be/k5A26Sc63F0]

sq3r: C.13.g. [Reading notes based on the SQ3R method: http://bit.ly/2U8V9la]

commute: D.1. What is your approximate commuting time from the hostel or from where you reside on most days? - 1 = Less than 15 minutes, 2 = 15 to 30 minutes, 3 = 30 minutes to 1 hour, 4 = Greater than 1 hour
study_time: D.2. How much time do you spend studying for the course per week? - 1 = Less than 2 hours, 2 = 2 to 5 hours, 3 = 3 to 5 hours, 4 = 4 to 10 hours
repeats_since_Y1: D.3. How many repeats have you had since 1st year?
paid_tuition: D.4. Do you receive extra paid tuition related to the course? - Yes = 1, No = 0
free_tuition: D.5. Do you receive extra free tuition related to the course? - Yes = 1, No = 0
club_extra_curricular: D.6. Do you participate in club-related extra-curricular activities in/outside the University?
sports_extra_curricular: D.7. Do you participate in sports-related extra-curricular activities in/outside the University?
exercise_per_week: D.8. How many times per week do you exercise? - 0 = 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times
meditate: D.9. How many times per week do you meditate? - 0 = 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times
pray: D.10. How many times per week do you pray? - 0 = 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times

laptop: D.12. Do you have access to a personal (not shared) laptop?

friendships: D.14. How would you rate the quality of your friendships?
romantic_relationships: D.15. How would you rate the quality of your romantic relationships? - 0 - I am not in any romantic relationship, 1 - Very bad, 2 - Bad, 3 - Good, 4 - Excellent
spiritual_wellnes: D.16. How would you rate the quality of your spiritual wellness?
financial_wellness: D.17. How would you rate the quality of your financial wellness?
health: D.18. How would you rate the quality of your health status?
day_out: D.19. How many times per week do you go out with friends (during the day)? - 0 - 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times
night_out: D.20. How many times per week do you go out with friends (at night)? - 0 - 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times
alcohol_or_narcotics: D.21. How many times per week do you drink alcohol or consume narcotics? - 0 - 0 times, 1 = 1 to 3 times, 2 = 4 to 6 times, 3 = More than 6 times

mentor_meetings: D.23. How many times per quarter (every 3 months) do you meet with your mentor? - 0 = 0 times, 1 = Less than 2 times, 2 = 2 to 3 times, 3 = More than 3 times
